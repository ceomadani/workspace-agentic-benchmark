Measure the workspace,

12 pillars · 11 cross-cutting extensions · L0–L4 maturity · vendor-neutral.

The output of an agentic system is bounded by the multiplication of capability (the model) and α (the workspace). LLM benchmarks measure capability. This benchmark measures α — the structural quality of the environment the agent operates in.

output = α(workspace) × capability(LLM)
α = √(quality × quantity) × density_indices
